

# 1️⃣ Messy

**Messy = scattered, unpredictable, or full of noise**

### Thanglish:

> “Data straight forward ah illa, random errors, exceptions, or confusing patterns irukku”

### Example: Customer buying behavior

* Customer A buys milk every week
* Customer B buys milk only sometimes
* Customer C buys milk twice a month irregularly

👉 Data la **pattern clear ah illa** → this is **messy**

---

# 2️⃣ Non-linear

**Non-linear = Relationship straight line-la illa**

### Thanglish:

> “Input increase aana output proportionally increase aagathu — curve mathiri behave pannum”

### Example: House price vs size

* Small increase in area → big price jump in city center
* Same increase in rural area → small price jump

👉 Price vs area **straight line-la illa** → this is **non-linear**

---

# ⚖️ Messy + Non-linear together

* Real-world data is often **messy** (noisy, irregular)
* And **non-linear** (complex relationships)

👉 That’s why **traditional rules fail** and **ML is needed**
